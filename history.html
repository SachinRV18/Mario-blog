Mario's Journey Through Time

Explore the fascinating history of Mario, from his humble beginnings in 1981 to the latest titles in the series!

1981 - Donkey Kong

Mario, initially called "Jumpman," made his first appearance in the arcade game Donkey Kong, where he had to save Pauline from a giant ape.

1985 - Super Mario Bros.

Super Mario Bros. was released for the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES), setting a new standard for platformer games worldwide.

1996 - Super Mario 64

Super Mario 64 revolutionized platformers, being one of the first games to offer full 3D gameplay and changing the future of video games.

2007 - Super Mario Galaxy

Super Mario Galaxy introduced gravity-defying mechanics and a space-themed world, earning praise for its creativity and gameplay innovation.

2017 - Super Mario Odyssey

Super Mario Odyssey brought Mario back to an open-world, sandbox-style adventure with new mechanics like "Cappy," his sentient hat.
